#  About Me:
I’m a technology risk professional with a background in cybersecurity, mathematics, and data analysis, with a strong interest in applying data science and quantitative methods to security and business problems.<br><br>My work sits at the intersection of cyber risk management and data-driven analysis. I’m particularly interested in how statistical modelling, simulations, and analytics can be used to better understand complex systems, quantify uncertainty, and support risk-based decision making.<br><br>I studied Mathematics and Statistics, which heavily shapes how I approach problems: breaking them down, analysing patterns in data, and building models that help explain or simulate real-world outcomes.<br><br>This GitHub is where I document projects I’m exploring around data analysis, risk quantification, and applied security analytics.<br><br><br>Technologies and tools I currently work with include:<br><br>Python (Pandas, NumPy)<br><br>SQL<br><br>Excel for advanced data analysis<br><br>Statistical modelling and simulation<br><br>Cybersecurity risk analysis<br><br>I enjoy working on projects that involve data exploration, modelling uncertainty, analysing patterns in real-world datasets, and translating technical or risk concepts into quantitative insights.
